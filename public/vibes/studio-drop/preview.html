STUDIO — A FOUR-PERSON PRESS

Atelier

Studio Drop is a four-person type studio working out of a converted printer's shop on Rua de Santa Catarina. We design one typeface family a year, run a small letterpress on Tuesdays, and ship merchandise four times a year to keep the lights on. The drops let us argue about kerning in public.

The kit on this page — paper cream, ink black, one ruthless red — is the same kit we apply to the studio identity, to the type specimen books, to the website you're reading. Every product is a footnote to that system.
