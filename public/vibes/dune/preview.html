— Volume IV · Of Arrakis · Folio 187

House Atreides

ON THE NATURE OF
spice AND ITS PROPHETS

— transcribed Princess Irulan · 10191 A.G.

Arrakis teaches the attitude of the knife — chopping off what is incomplete and saying: now it is complete, because it ends here. Spice is the great sieve through which the Imperium's commerce, its navigation, and its prescience all pass. Without it, no Guild navigator can fold space; without it, no Bene Gesserit can read the bloodlines; without it, the Padishah Emperor has no Empire.

The Fremen call it melange when refined — the substance that opens the eye of the universe. Their water-discipline, their stillsuits, their reverence for Shai-Hulud, all are inseparable from the cycle of spice on the great desert.

"FEAR IS THE mind-killer."
